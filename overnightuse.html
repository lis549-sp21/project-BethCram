For marine state parks, instead of an annual discover pass, an annual moorage pass is your pass to use the state park docks or buoys. Fees are due year round for moorage at docks, floats, & buoys for use Users have about 30 minutes to submit payment upon arrival but that is up to the ranger. The annual moorage pass and discovery pass are not interchangable. All use of washington state parks facilities require fee payments. These payments support ongoing parks projects, seasonal staff, and amenities provided at parks. Mooring buoys are first come first serve and $15 - it is always a good idea to ensure the buoy is holding your vessel as intended. You are responsible for your vessel. If you decide to pay per use the offical current rates and fees can be found here and annual moorage permits here and camping rates here

Boat Rates: Rates and fees as of 04/20/2022 $15 for buoys $0.70/foot - $15 minimum fee for daily fee on docks and floats $5/foot - minimum $60 to purchase an annual moorage permit

Vessels over 45 feet are not permitted on buoys
