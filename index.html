Know before you go; Washington State Parks

Unoffical tips from a Park Aide

All information contained within these pages are entirely my own thoughts and opinions are not those of my employer, Washington State Parks.

Washington State Parks has a plethora of parks available to the public to visit. This page is going to focus on how to plan a trip to a marine state park in the San Juan Region.

These parks include; Blind Island, James Island, Spencer Spit, Lime Kiln Point, Turn Island, Jones Island, Posey Island, Stuart Island, Patos Island, Sucia Island, Matia Island, Clark Island, Moran, Obstruction Pass, Saddlebag Island, Doe Island, and Burrows Island.
If you're going to plan a day trip, visit this page for trip tips. If you're going to plan an overnight trip, visit this page trip tips.
